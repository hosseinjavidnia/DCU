7. Final Challenge: Reusability

        

            You also downloaded sales_data.csv. This file has completely different columns (price, quantity) than the student files.
            If you hard-coded row['exam_score'] inside your loader, it will fail on the sales data.
        


        

            

                
The Problem

                Your loader probably looks like this:
                
row['exam_score'] = int(row['exam_score'])

                This crashes if the file doesn't have an exam_score!
            

            

                
The Challenge

                Make your loader Generic.
                

                Remove the specific type conversion from the loader. Just return the strings.
                


                Perform the conversion (String -> Int) outside the loader, in your analysis steps.
